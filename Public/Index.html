FAQ

The questions
founders actually ask.

We already have good SEO — isn't that enough?
+
SEO optimises for Google's PageRank algorithm. AI commerce is an entirely separate discovery layer. ChatGPT doesn't use PageRank — it uses structured data, semantic product feeds, and knowledge graphs. You can rank #1 on Google and be invisible to every AI shopping agent. These are different infrastructures requiring different solutions.
How do I know this actually works? Can you prove it?
+
Run the free Pulse audit first — zero commitment required. You'll see exactly where AI is ignoring you, with your score benchmarked against your category average. That data speaks for itself before any money changes hands. Our case studies also show specific Pulse score improvements, AI citation counts, and revenue attribution numbers — not vague claims.
Isn't it too early to invest in AI commerce?
+
Early is exactly the point. ChatGPT Shopping launched in 2024. Perplexity Commerce is live. Amazon Rufus is active inside the world's largest store. The window for first-mover advantage in your category is approximately 12 months. Brands that build AI infrastructure now will own their category in AI search by 2026. Those who wait will pay significantly more to catch up — if the window is still open.
We don't have technical resources in-house.
+
That's exactly why we exist. 100% done-for-you delivery. One onboarding call, one review call, weekly updates. We handle schema implementation, semantic markup, LLM feeds, knowledge graphs, and citation optimisation. Zero founder technical time required beyond the initial onboarding. The Pulse audit itself takes 2 minutes.
What happens to the work if we stop?
+
All IP belongs to you — not us. Every schema, every product feed, every knowledge graph we build is yours to keep. Month-to-month retainers mean no lock-in. Clean handover documentation is provided on exit. The infrastructure you've built doesn't disappear — it just stops being updated. Which is why most clients stay.
How is this different from an SEO agency?
+
SEO agencies are optimising for a channel that is declining — traditional search is being replaced by AI Overviews and direct AI shopping. We're building infrastructure for the channel that's replacing it. We don't compete with your SEO agency — we're solving a problem they're not equipped to handle, using technical skills (semantic architecture, RAG systems, LLM optimisation) that don't exist in traditional agencies.
